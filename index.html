This Week in the War Room

Winning the First 30 Seconds

Weekly Leadership Focus

Top producers use the first 30 seconds to establish credibility, lower resistance, and earn the next question. This week, agents must sound calm, clear, and in control.

Training Topic

Use a clean opener and transition into discovery: “Hi, this is [Name] with Our 365 Financial Solution Agency. I’m following up because you recently requested information about coverage options, and I wanted to take a quick minute to see what you’re currently looking for.” Then ask whether they are mainly looking for better coverage, lower cost, or more clarity on options.

Practice Focus

Objection of the Week: “I’m not interested.” Practice this response: “I understand. A lot of people say that at first because they do not want to waste time unless there is actually something worth looking at. Let me ask you one quick question.”
